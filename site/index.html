Marker

Select text. It's already copied.

  • Works system-wide: native apps, browsers, Telegram, terminals.
  • Selected text goes straight to your clipboard, ready to ⌘ V. Prefer your clipboard untouched? Turn that off: selections stay inside Marker, and ⌥ V pastes the latest.
  • Selected something yesterday and lost it? It's still in the menu bar: search it or browse by app.
